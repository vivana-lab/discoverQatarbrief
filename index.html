Discover Qatar

Discover Qatar (Arabic:اكتشف قطر, aiktashaf Qatar) is the Destination Management division of Qatar Airways Company Q.C.S.C., that holds exclusive contracts with all of Qatar’s 4 and 5-star hotels, and offers more than 40 tours and excursions.[1] Headquartered in the Qatar Airways Tower 3 in Doha [2], the division assures business partnerships under strict Governance and set regulations of one of the world’s leading airlines. Composed of a team of tourism experts and premium service providers, Discover Qatar offers Accommodation, Meet & Assist, Stopover, Transfers, Tours & Excursions, Transit Tours and other flexible services at exclusive prices.

Vision

The division's vision is to be the top destination management company in Qatar through constant product innovation, delivering unique and exclusive experiences according to customers’ needs & aspirations.

Mission

The Company's goal is to continue to advance Qatar as a premium tourist destination by creating and delivering unique and unforgettable experiences of Qatar, curated for their partners to include exclusive offers, an unremitting focus on quality and always with a customer-first approach. To operate with a commitment to sustainability, ensuring a complete respect to the heritage and culture of the country as well as to their stakeholders and broader community.

Tours

1. Discover Doha

Frequency: Daily | Duration: 4 hours

Guests can discover the unique heritage of Downtown Doha as they wander through the vibrant labyrinth of alleyways of Souq Waqif. [5] They will be able to view modern day Doha’s towering iconic architecture and discover the Corniche - Doha’s iconic waterfront where the azure waters of the Arabian Gulf provides the setting to the city’s impressive skyline.

2. Discover Souq Waqif

Frequency: Daily, excluding public holidays | Duration: 2 hours

Guests can discover the hidden gems of the Souq Waqif through a unique cultural shopping experience. [6] The tour offers a journey through the ancient marketplace, masterfully redeveloped to maintain its original 19th-century look. Guests will explore the vibrant alleys with an expert local guide to find the best vendors and deals, while learning about Qatari culture and history along the way. Souq Waqif is one of the most distinctively cultural attractions to explore in Qatar and a perfect encapsulation of local culture. The local Qatari guide will take guests to some of the hidden gems of Souq Waqif including the Spice & Gold Souq, Falcon and Carpet markets. This is the perfect trip for first time visitors to Qatar and the best way to experience authentic Qatari culture. Guests can expect a walking tour through the Spice Market, Perfume Market, Handicraft Market, Art Centre and Falcon Market.

3. Discover Arabic Coffee traditions

Frequency: Daily, excluding public holidays | Duration: 2 hours

Guests will discover the ancient coffee traditions of Qatar and experience true Arabian hospitality. Arabic coffee (pronounced “gahwa” in the Arabic dialect) [7] has been a central part of Arab culture for centuries and its preparation and serving is marked by elaborate traditions and rituals. Tour guide will escort the guests to the Cultural House located at the beach side Souq Waqif Al Wakra, where guests will experience one of Qatar's oldest traditions. The guide will explain the history and significance of Arabic coffee and the elaborate etiquette for the server, the guest and the host. By the end of the tour guests will have achieved an intimate knowledge of the history of coffee in the region and a deeper understanding of the ceremony's link to the heritage of Qatar's hospitality and etiquette.

4. Discover Qatar by Sky


Frequency: Daily (1 November - 31 March) | Duration: 3 hours

The experience of a lifetime awaits guests with spectacular hot air balloon flights. Guests are taken to the skies in a hot air balloon and can enjoy panoramic views over the city or desert and experience Qatar from the air. Guests should be ready before sunrise as they will be taken to the sky to see the sun lifting behind a dramatic land or cityscape. [8] Back on land, guests are treated with a delightful breakfast to end a truly exhilarating experience.

5. Discover dunes and the Inland Sea

Frequency: Daily | Duration: 4 hours

Guests will discover the remarkable desert landscape where sand dunes spill into the azure waters of Arabian Peninsula. Qualified desert drivers will take them on a life time adventure, traveling through the dramatic sand dunes to Khor Al Adaid, a large tidal embayment recognized for its outstanding natural heritage value. [9] Guests will have an opportunity to take in the magnificent desert landscape, visit the towering Sheraton Dune and marvel at the azure waters of the Arabian Sea before they depart for a brief comfort stop at our desert camp on the way back to Doha.

6. Discover Regional Cuisine

Frequency: Daily, excluding public holidays | Duration: 4 hours

Guests will discover the hidden gems of the Souq Waqif through a unique cultural experience and local food journey. Taste their way through the back alleys of Souq Waqif and learn more about regional traditions while experiencing true Arabian hospitality. [10] Their local Qatari guide will share a wealth of information from local dress and customs to trade, traditions and more. Guests will visit different parts of the souq where a range of items such as perfumes, fabric and even falcons are traded. This immersive cultural experience will leave them with an in depth understanding of life and culture in Qatar.

Airport meet and assist


The service can be booked online or at Al Maha services desk at the airport. Each journey to Qatar begins and ends at Hamad International Airport. [11] Meet and Assist service is an exclusive experience that personalizes guests’ arrival, departure and transit journey from/to Hamad International Airport. Private transfers offer seamless door-to-door transfers for customers in all cabin classes. Guests can choose from standard and luxury vehicles options and make every journey safe and reliable with Discover Qatar.

Welcome Home Hotel Quarantine packages


Discover Qatar facilitates Welcome Home Hotel quarantine packages in accordance with MOPH Travel and Return policies.[12][13][14]
